## The HOA

The Pine Valley HOA is a voluntary organization. Membership is not required, and the HOA has no authority to enforce community rules or standards.

The HOA exists for two purposes: maintaining the landscaping and greenery in the entrance island at the neighborhood entrance, and bringing neighbors together through neighborhood-wide social events.
